noon: Ad Monetization Platform Integration

Tagsnoon
TLDRNoon identified an opportunity to enhance on-site advertising and create a new revenue stream, addressing challenges like inefficient ad space use and lack of robust reporting. They integrated Flipkart's Admon service, involving solution design, technical integration, user experience design, and operational processes. Context and Challenge

At noon, our growth and product teams identified an opportunity to enhance our on-site advertising capabilities and create a new revenue stream. Key challenges included:

  1. Inefficient use of on-site advertising space.
  1. Lack of robust reporting for brands on their advertising performance.
  1. Limited ability for brands to effectively use their marketing budgets on our platform.
  1. Inaccurate data from existing asset IDs and widget IDs for banner ads.

Approach and Actions

To address these challenges, we initiated a project to integrate Flipkart's Admon service with noon's backend. The approach included:

  1. Solution Design:
    • Planned integration of Flipkart Admon with noon's backend for more robust bidding and selling of on-site space.
    • Designed a system to provide brands with effective reporting on bidded slot performance.
  1. Technical Integration:
    • Coordinated the building of noon's AdMon module and test API.
    • Planned rendering of ad modules and event tracking setup (impressions, click-through, viewability).
  1. User Experience Design:
    • Designed new ad modules with clear "Sponsored" indicators to maintain transparency.
    • Planned for seamless integration of ads into existing page layouts.
  1. Operational Process:
    • Established a workflow for the on-site merchandising team to work with Flipkart's service team for campaign setup and reporting.
  1. Measurement Framework:
    • Defined clear metrics for ad performance, including viewable impressions based on industry standards.

Specific Actions Taken

  1. Technical Implementation:
    • Created a new Node.js repository (ads-api) with Docker setup for deployment.
    • Integrated Flipkart Admon API to fetch ad content based on slot IDs and user device information.
    • Implemented front-end integration for ad rendering in product catalogs.
  1. Ad Serving Process:
    • Established a system for noon's on-site team to send campaign details (creative assets, links, slot names) to Flipkart's service team.
    • Implemented the serving of ad content through noon's AdMon module based on data received from Flipkart.
  1. Reporting System:
    • Set up a process for Flipkart to provide day-level reporting of slots and campaigns to noon's on-site team.
  1. User Experience:
    • Designed ad modules with clear "Sponsored" or "AD" indicators to maintain transparency.
    • Ensured seamless integration of ads into existing page layouts to maintain user experience.

Expected Results and Impact

  1. New Revenue Stream:
    • Enable brands to bid on on-site advertising slots, creating an additional revenue stream for noon.
    • This resulted in a 2M USD revenue achievement during Yellow Friday Sale of 2020
  1. Improved Brand Partnerships:
    • Provide brands with more effective ways to use their marketing budgets on the platform.
    • Offer robust reporting on ad performance to demonstrate value to brand partners.
  1. Enhanced User Experience:
    • Implement clearly marked sponsored content to maintain transparency with users.
    • Integrate ads seamlessly into existing layouts to minimize disruption to the shopping experience.
  1. Scalability:
    • Lay the groundwork for future expansion into more biddable on-site assets, such as top product boxes in search results and product carousels.

Lessons Learned and Future Plans

  1. Third-Party Integration: Leveraging a specialized third-party service (Flipkart Admon) allowed for quicker implementation of advanced ad serving and reporting capabilities.
  1. Phased Approach: Starting with a managed service model allowed for quicker launch, with plans to transition to self-service in future versions.
  1. Standards Compliance: Adopting industry standards for metrics like viewable impressions ensures alignment with advertiser expectations and compatibility with broader digital advertising ecosystems.
  1. Cross-Functional Collaboration: Success depended on close coordination between product, engineering, on-site merchandising, and external partners (Flipkart).
  1. Future Expansion: The project laid the groundwork for future enhancements, including biddable slots in search results and product carousels, demonstrating forward-thinking product strategy.
